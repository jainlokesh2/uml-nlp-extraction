Guests are required to book rooms according to their preferences and availability. Upon arrival, both Guests and the Receptionist engage in the check-in process, ensuring a smooth start to the accommodation experience. Similarly, the check-out procedure involves both Guests and the Receptionist, aimed at concluding the stay with a streamlined and satisfactory process. The Manager holds the responsibility of managing reservations, overseeing room allocations, and ensuring optimal occupancy and guest satisfaction.
