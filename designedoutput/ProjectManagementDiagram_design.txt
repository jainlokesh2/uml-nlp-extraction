The Manager is tasked with the creation of projects, setting the foundation for structured and goal-oriented progress. Additionally, the Manager is responsible for the assignment of tasks, ensuring that each team member has clear responsibilities and deliverables. Stakeholders are involved in the generation of reports, facilitating transparency and informed decision-making. Team members are expected to collaborate effectively, fostering a cohesive and productive working environment. They are also responsible for reviewing progress, ensuring that the project stays on track and aligns with the set objectives.
